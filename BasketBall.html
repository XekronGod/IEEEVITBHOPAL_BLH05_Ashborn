Sports Technology in Basketball - Paris 2024 Olympics

Advanced Basketballs

At the Paris 2024 Olympics, basketballs are equipped with the latest technology to improve grip, durability, and performance. These balls feature enhanced grip textures and advanced composite materials for consistent bounce and control.

Additionally, some basketballs have embedded sensors that track ball trajectory, spin rate, and impact, providing valuable data for players and coaches.

Smart Court Systems

The courts used in the Paris 2024 Olympics are equipped with smart technology to enhance game analysis and officiating. These systems include sensors embedded in the court surface and overhead cameras to track player movements, ball position, and game dynamics.

The data collected helps referees make accurate calls and provides detailed statistics for teams to analyze performance and improve strategies.

Player Performance Analytics

Advanced player tracking technologies are used to monitor and analyze performance metrics such as speed, acceleration, and shooting accuracy. Wearable sensors and tracking devices provide real-time data on player movement and exertion.

Coaches and players use this data to optimize training regimens, improve individual and team performance, and gain a competitive edge.
