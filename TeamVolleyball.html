Sports Technology in Team Volleyball - Paris 2024 Olympics

Advanced Volleyballs

The volleyballs used at the Paris 2024 Olympics are engineered with advanced materials to enhance durability and performance. These balls feature improved aerodynamic designs and high-quality synthetic leather to provide better grip and control.

The new design also helps in maintaining the ball's shape and weight, ensuring consistency in play under various conditions.

Smart Court Systems

The courts at the Paris 2024 Olympics are equipped with smart technology to enhance game analysis and officiating. These systems include sensors embedded in the court surface to track ball trajectory, player movements, and boundary line accuracy.

The data collected helps referees make accurate calls and provides teams with valuable insights for strategy and performance improvement.

Player Tracking Technologies

Player tracking technologies, including wearables and motion sensors, are used to monitor athlete performance in real-time. These technologies capture data on player speed, jump height, and movement patterns, providing coaches with detailed performance metrics.

This information helps in fine-tuning training programs and enhancing tactical approaches during matches.
