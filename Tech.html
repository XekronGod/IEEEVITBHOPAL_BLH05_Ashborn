Common Sports Technologies in Paris 2024 Olympics

Motion Capture Systems

Motion capture systems are used to track and analyze athletes' movements with high precision. These systems involve placing sensors on the athletes to capture their movements and provide detailed data for performance analysis and improvement.

This technology assists in refining techniques, reducing injuries, and enhancing overall performance.

VR Training

Virtual Reality (VR) training systems are being utilized across various sports to enhance athletes' skills and strategies. These systems create immersive environments for training, allowing athletes to practice scenarios that they might encounter in real competitions.

VR training helps in improving reaction times, strategic thinking, and mental preparedness.

High-Speed Cameras

High-speed cameras are employed to capture fast-paced actions in sports with great detail. These cameras provide slow-motion footage that is crucial for analyzing and improving athletes' techniques and performance.

The slow-motion analysis helps coaches and athletes to observe and correct movements that happen too quickly to see in real-time.
